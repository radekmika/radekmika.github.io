In my opinion, you are looking for someone, who…

  • Knows how web apps work and knows how to code. And should be a quick learner.
  • Has analytical mind and likes to figure out how things work or why they don’t work as expected.
  • Is by purpose nice to people. And is great communicator, so he/she can explain even complicated things in a simple language.
  • Understands which things might be critical and which can wait.
  • Can work remotely. Knows how to manage his time and is responsible.
  • Doesn’t need managers to tell him what to do, because he/she is motivated by being able to improve something he/she admires.
  • Someone who understands your values and the way you work.
